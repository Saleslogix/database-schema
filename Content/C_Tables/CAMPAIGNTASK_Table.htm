CAMPAIGNTASK Table

Added in Version 6.2

The CAMPAIGNTASK table stores data related to the tasks associated with marketing campaigns. Tasks are assignments, such as writing the draft copy for a newspaper advertisementor sending a direct mail piece to the printer, that require owners. Owners can be Saleslogix users or outside vendors. Owners are assigned to tasks in the Saleslogix Client using the Manage Campaigns dialog box.
