ADHOCGROUP Table

The AdHocGroup table stores the definitions for ad hoc groups that are created in the Saleslogix Client. Ad hoc groups are randomly selected groups of accounts, contacts, or opportunities that do not meet any specific condition. Other groups are stored in the Plugin table.
