NOTIFICATIONCONFIG Table

The NOTIFICATIONCONFIG table stores information on notification methods such as MAPI, Internet POP3/SMTP, VIMl, and so on.

CreateUser and ModifyUser are foreign keys referencing UserID in the USERINFO table.
