RMA Table

The RMA table stores information on Return agreements with customers. This information is entered using the Saleslogix Client and includes the Account ID, shipping instructions such as how to ship, who to ship to, submitting date, person submitting, and other details.

  • The RMA table has related tables for products received and products shipped.
