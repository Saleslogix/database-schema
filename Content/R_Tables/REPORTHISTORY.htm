REPORTHISTORY Table

Added in Version 8.1

The REPORTHISTORY table stores the history for reporting job executions.

 

  • While this table does not have a field defined as a primary key, the REPORTHISTORYIID field should be unique and considered a primary key.

  • CreateUser and ModifyUser are foreign keys referencing UserID in the USERINFO table.
