PROCEDURECONTRIBUTOR Table

The PROCEDURECONTRIBUTOR table stores information about individuals who have contributed details, steps, or similar information, to Saleslogix procedures.

table relationships

The PROCEDURECONTRIBUTOR table has a (Many) to (Zero or One) relationship with the PROCEDURES table.

CreateUser and ModifyUser are foreign keys referencing UserID in the USERINFO table.
