SALESORDERDETAIL Table

Deprecated in Version 7.0

The SALESORDERDETAIL table stores detailed product information about sales orders and quotes, such as quantity, unit, and cost information.

CreateUser and ModifyUser are foreign keys referencing UserID in the USERINFO table.
